COVID-19

Coronavirus is a virus that is common among animals. It is a dangerous disease with incubation period between 4-6 days . It is fatal especially for those with weakened immune system,the elderly and the young. Is a very dangerous disease that has killed millions of people.

SYMPTOMS

Covid-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness. There are many symptoms. Namely: fever,runny nose,cough,sore throat,dry cough,tiredness,headache,loss of taste, aches and pains.

PREVENTIONS

To prevent yourself, you need to wash your hands with soap and water before touching anything including your nose, eyes and mouth ,covering your mouth and nose when coughing and sneezing etc.
